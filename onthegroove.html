PROJECT TITLE:

On The Groove

CREATIVE CODE WEB DESIGN BRANDING & MARKETING DESIGN

On The Groove is a speculative campaign by Spotify, in partnership with TFL, designed to connect London Underground commuters through music.

Through ‘tapping into’ NFC stations, commuters can share their Recently Played tracks, explore ‘Music Maps’ of different tube lines, and listen to random songs chosen by fellow passengers.
